Welcome.

This is a webpage dedicated to the MPIC project. You can find my current progress on the project at Github.

Here you'll find definitions and inner workings of MPI code, the MPI compiler, and the standard library. You'll need to learn the C language, and Assembly (FASM syntax) to understand the 'meta' parts of the specification. Click one of the links on the sidebar or just below this text to get started.
